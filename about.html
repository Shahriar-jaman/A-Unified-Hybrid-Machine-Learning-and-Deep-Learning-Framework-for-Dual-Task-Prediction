About us

AIMHESS – AI That Saves Futures

Our Mission

Every year, thousands of students struggle within a hidden, bidirectional cycle where academic decline and psychological distress fuel one another.
We built this framework to break this cycle by simultaneously predicting academic pass/fail outcomes and psychological stress levels from a single, unified platform.

Powered by advanced machine learning and deep learning architectures trained on a massive dataset of 200,000 student records, our system delivers up to 88.82% predictive accuracy. By integrating an intelligent conversational chatbot for personalized guidance and an adaptive daily planner that updates based on performance and stress levels, we provide actionable, explainable support to safeguard student futures.
